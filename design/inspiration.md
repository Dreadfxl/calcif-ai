# Calcif.ai Design Inspiration & Glossary

## References
- Brutalist architecture (Le Corbusier, Tadao Ando)
- Y2K web: Winamp, iMacs, MSN Messenger, early Flash
- Sci-fi: Matrix (UI effects), Blade Runner, Ghost in the Shell
- Digital art: Beeple, GMUNK, Ash Thorp

## Glossary
- **Calcify**: (verb) To become rigid or solid; here refers to tasks "set in stone" on completion.
- **Bucket**: Custom task category/classification.
- **Dog bone**: Visual metaphor for completed/categorized task.
- **Brutalism**: Design with emphasis on raw structure, geometry, unapologetic aesthetics.
- **Matrix Rain**: Animated vertical code, 2000s digital art reference.
- **Y2K**: Early 2000s, glossy, retro-futuristic, color-rich computer style.
- **Neon Glow**: Bright, colored lighting on elements, often animated.
